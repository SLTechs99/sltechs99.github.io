Our Services

On-Grid Solar System

On-grid solar systems work only when it is connected with grid. Excess power generated will be sent to grid and paid back.

Off-Grid Solar System

Off-grid solar systems work independently of the grid but have batteries which can store the solar power generated by the system.

Hybrid Solar System

Hybrid solar systems are connected with both batteries and grid, excess power generated will be stored in batteries first rather than sending to grid.

Solar Street Lights

Solar Street lights are powered by solar panels. Solar panels charge a rechargeable battery, which powers a LED lamp during the night.

Solar Water Heater

Solar water heater is a device that captures sunlight to heat water. It is an economical way to generate hot water for your family.

Solar surveillance Camera

Solar surveillance cameras are powered by solar panels and it can be operated and monitored using mobile devices.

Solar Water Pumps

Solar Pumps are electrical pumps draws water from the open well, bore well, stream, pond, canal etc.

EV Charging Station

EV charging stations use the power generated by solar panels to charge the Electric vehicles.

Operations & Maintanence

We support periodic maintanence of Solar system to increase its life time and performance.
